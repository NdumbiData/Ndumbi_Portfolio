This Kaggle dataset includes TV shows and movies available on Netflix as of 2019, sourced from Flixable, a third-party Netflix search engine. A 2018 report highlighted that while Netflix’s movie count has dropped by over 2,000 titles since 2010, the number of TV shows has almost tripled. Potential Insights: Analyze content across different countries Identify similar titles using text features Conduct network analysis on actors and directors Explore Netflix’s focus shift from movies to TV shows Merging with external data like IMDb or Rotten Tomatoes could yield further findings.
